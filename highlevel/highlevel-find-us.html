Getting to Highlevel Diner

From Whyte Ave:

We're just a short walk or drive from Whyte Ave. Head south on 109 Street, turn right on 88 Ave NW and you'll find us on the left side. Look for the yellow building — you can't miss it.

From the LRT:

Take the Valley Line LRT to the Mill Creek-Bonnie Doon station. Head west along 88 Ave NW for about 10 minutes and Highlevel Diner will be on your right.

From Rideshares:

Rideshares like Uber and Lyft can drop you off right at our door. Simply enter "Highlevel Diner, 10912 88 Ave NW, Edmonton" as your destination and you'll be here in no time.

Convenient Street Parking:

Street parking is available along 88 Ave NW and the surrounding side streets. You'll find plenty of spots nearby, making it easy to park and enjoy your visit.

On-Site Parking:

We also offer on-site parking for our customers. Pull into our dedicated parking area and you'll be steps away from a delicious meal.
